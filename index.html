About Diana

I'm Diana — a registered nurse with a business background and a deep passion for holistic wellness.

After overcoming personal health challenges like hypothyroidism and Bell's palsy, I've learned that real strength is about showing up every day — for your body, your mind, and your dreams.

I combine clinical insight, business acumen, and a passion for fitness to inspire others to find clarity, balance, and purpose in their lives. Whether it's at the gym, through my studies, or advocating mental resilience, my goal is simple: live well and uplift those around me.
